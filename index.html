About the App

Zone Audit helps you map every task in your work life across two dimensions: how much energy it gives you, and how much value it creates. Based on time and energy frameworks taught by leading business coaches, it helps you identify your Zone of Genius — and protect it.

Stop grinding on tasks that drain you. Start spending your best hours on your best work.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the HB-HV / LB-LV framework?

Tasks are mapped across two axes: Energy Boost (HB = High Boost, LB = Low Boost) and Value (HV = High Value, LV = Low Value). Your goal is to maximize time in the HB-HV quadrant — tasks that energize you and move the needle. Tasks in the LB-LV quadrant should be dropped if possible.

Why are two quadrants called traps?

HB-LV tasks feel productive because they energize you — but they create little real value. LB-HV tasks feel necessary because they are important — but they slowly drain you. Spending too much time in either leads to burnout or stagnation.

Is my data stored anywhere?

All your data is stored locally on your device. Zone Audit does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.
